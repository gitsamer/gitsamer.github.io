2. Service Description

Kado is a food product scanning and nutrition analysis application that allows users to:

  • Scan food product barcodes
  • Capture and analyze nutrition facts tables using OCR
  • Calculate personalized health scores based on nutrition data
  • Track food consumption history
  • Receive health insights and recommendations

All nutrition data processing occurs locally on your device or through secure cloud services for maximum privacy.

3. Data Privacy & Processing

Your Privacy is Our Priority:

  • Scanned nutrition labels are processed using OCR technology
  • Product data is retrieved from the Open Database
  • Your personal health profile (age, weight, etc.) is stored locally
  • Scan history is stored on your device and can be deleted anytime
  • We do not sell or share your personal health information
  • Product images are not permanently stored unless you choose to save them

For complete details, see our Privacy Policy.
