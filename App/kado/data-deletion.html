Kado - Data Deletion Request

At Kado, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your Kado account and all associated data, please send an email to:

support.kado@balshoo.com
Please include the following information in your email:
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your Kado account
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • Any additional account identifiers if applicable

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your personal information (name, email, profile data)
  • Your health preferences and dietary restrictions
  • Your scan history and saved products
  • Any custom notes or preferences
  • All associated user profiles (if using Family plan)

Processing Timeline

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Important Notes:
  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • If you have an active subscription, it will be cancelled
  • Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory compliance purposes for a limited time
  • Anonymized usage data for analytics may be retained

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or our privacy practices, please contact us at support.kado@balshoo.com
